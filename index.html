Everyday there are many UFO sightings reported in the United States and around the world. This interactive map is a time series of UFO sightings from the year 2015. The sightings can be explored in two different ways: grouped by state per quarter of the year or every sighting for the entire year by month. The remote control is designed to cycle through the different quarters of the year or start an animation to display the individual sightings by month.
